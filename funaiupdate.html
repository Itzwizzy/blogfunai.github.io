*EBONYI STATE UNION UNITED KINGDOM SCHOLARSHIP ANNOUNCEMENT*

*Scholarships! Scholarships!! Scholarships!!!*

Please listen to this special announcement: Ebonyi State Union United Kingdom and Ireland wishes to announce that it is now receiving applications for its 2023/2024 Undergraduate Scholarships. ESU UK wishes to award one-off scholarship grants to *indigent 100 Level Undergraduate students from Ebonyi State.* The award will be given to selected students from each Local Government Areas of Ebonyi State. Applications are therefore welcome from interested Ebonyi State indigenes who have recently gained admission, (100 level students only) to study any course in any of the Universities in Nigeria for the 2023/2024 academic session.

*Application Criteria*

  1. Must be an Indigene of Ebonyi State
  2. 100 Level Student with evidence of admission into a Nigerian University
  3. Indigent High performing students with the best results will be selected and awarded scholarships at the Union’s discretion.
    4.

*Deadline for the submission of application form is 31st July 2023.*

*Application Procedure:*
  1. Application form completion. To obtain this form, please email ebonyiukscholarships@gmail.com or visit www.ebonyiunion.co.uk.
  2. Submit evidence of Local Government of origin
  3. JAMB Results
  4. UTME/ Post UTME Result
  5. University Admission letter
  6. GCE or NECO Results
  7. Personal Statement explaining why you need to be awarded the scholarship.
  8. A scanned copy of passport-sized photograph showing applicant’s face.
    9.

*Application forms for ESU UK Scholarship is free!*

Signed;
  • Mr. Emmanuel Umoke (President, Ebonyi Union UK and Ireland)
  • Chief Patrick Oleh – Secretary
  • Dr. Nnanna Igwe – Ex President
  • Mr. Chima Olugh - Ex President
